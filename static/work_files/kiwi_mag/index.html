Kiwi Magazine is a bi-annual print publication serving to expose the human experience. It covers anything that contributors submit, which ranges from art & culture to politics.

Link to the site and the spring 2016 issue

Pauline and Zori consulted me a lot in the early stages of Kiwi; they were InDe members who decided to start a print magazine. I worked closely with them, sharing all the knowledge and experiences I had with starting InDe.

I sat on the board as Director of Design, and designed the website with their creative direction. I also played a role in the layout of the magazine, as well as contributing my own piece to the Spring Issue: MIXER.

I've been maintaining the site since the club's founding in Spring 2016.
