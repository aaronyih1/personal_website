Wit is an interdisciplinary design event that promotes cross-polination through collaboration; it hopes to boost creativity and empower atendees to create.

Link to original interactive site.

"Bringing together a variety of life experiences and contrasting perspectives results in a creative tension that often leads to more innovative and interesting ideas." — Tom & David Kelley

With this insight from Creative Confidence1, the idea for a design-centric event at UCLA was born. Having assembled a team of passionate designers and artists, we broke up into smaller tactical forces to conquer different parts of the event.

I led the design team, which worked to establish a stong brand identity for the event. We developed the logo and general style guide for the event, and we brought the website from sketches to mockups to the live site.

Ultimately, the event was cancelled due to lack of funding, but the event is being re-incarnated as "pixel" later this quarter.
