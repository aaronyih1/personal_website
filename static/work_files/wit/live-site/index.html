WIT is the first design event of its kind. Participating in it will engage you with other design enthusiasts and challenge your creative thinking. Throughout this two-day designathon, you'll learn to see the design process as accessible and exciting. Resources, knowledge, and experiences will be plentiful. With collaborative exercises that help you form your squad, WIT provides you the opportunity to question and solve real problems that the world faces.

Design is

COLLABORATIVE

VISUALLY ALLURING

FUNCTIONAL

Collaboration is at the heart of innovation. The integration of diverse perspectives and ideas creates a space of cross pollination, exhilaration, and vast creative potential.

Aesthetics are an essential part of every design because it is responsible for creating that initial attraction that draws in the audience of an object.

Functionality is as essential an element of design as aesthetics. Functional design promotes efficiency while serving a purpose and eliminating redundancies.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How does a designathon differ from a hackathon?
A designathon is more structured experience directed towards fostering a collaborative and innovative environment. It uses design methodology to produce creative solutions.
Do I need prior design skills or knowledge to register?
No, we welcome anyone interested in design, regardless of experience.
What should I bring?
Bring whatever you need to help you create, whether it be a laptop or sketchbook. A notebook and pen will be provided by WIT.
How much will it cost me?
Nothing.
What can my team create?
Anything. Examples include infographics, a PowerPoint pitch, or any other form of concept visualization.
Do I need a team to register?
A team is not necessary for registration. WIT is designed to help you meet like-minded individuals and encourages you to form teams with new acquaintances.
