UAS is a group of students dedicated to educating the UCLA community about Arab Americans and the Arab world, with a focus on humanitarian issues and culture.

Link to facebook page.

UAS was looking to build brand awareness at UCLA in order to better serve their mission of sharing Arab culture with the larger UCLA community.

As a part of this push towards a greater presence on campus, they wanted a t-shirt design that would unify all of its members and communate the club's core values.

I worked, as project lead, with 2 other designers from InDe to to complete all of the iterations shown on the left. The final version was printed and distributed to club members. Sometimes seeing the shirts around campus is cool.
